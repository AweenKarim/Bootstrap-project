What We Do

Excessive consumption and a disposable mindset have given rise to a worldwide waste dilemma. Despite the technical recyclability of even intricate waste, the majority of materials lack economic viability for recycling. Consequently, refuse accumulates in landfills, causing environmental pollution, while fresh resources are extracted from the Earth to produce new goods.

Our Impact

Apart from our complimentary recycling initiatives, we provide paid recycling options that individuals can acquire to support the #myReCyclemovement. The expenses for these solutions cover the collection box, transportation to and from your location to our recycling facilities, and the essential processes of sorting, cleaning, and recycling the contents.
